Case Highlights

Representative Deployments

University Campus

Diverts 1.5 tons/day dining hall waste. Frass fertilizes campus landscapes; research program quantifies soil gains.

  • 164 tons CO₂e avoided annually
  • Students engaged through curriculum and internships
  • Zero-waste certification achieved

Grocery Distribution Hub

Converts shrink and expired goods into larvae meal sold to partner feed mills.

  • $420K annual savings and revenue combined
  • 47% reduction in waste hauling frequency
  • ESG reporting integrated into corporate disclosures

Municipal Pilot

City diverts residential food waste, supplying frass to urban farms and community gardens.

  • Job training program for local residents
  • Grants leveraged for expansion
  • Community compost education series launched

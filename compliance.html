Assurance & Governance

Regulatory Alignment & Compliance Programs

Our valorization platform operates within comprehensive regulatory frameworks covering feed, fertilizer, worker safety, and environmental stewardship. We deliver documentation, training, and audit support from day one.

HACCP • GMP • ISO-Informed

Compliance Pillars

Integrated Frameworks

Food & Feed Safety

We operate HACCP and GMP programs aligned with FDA, AAFCO, and CFIA guidance.

  • Process flow hazard analysis with critical control points
  • Supplier verification and incoming material testing
  • Recall and traceability drills twice per year

Fertilizer Regulations

Frass products hold the necessary state registrations and labeling approvals.

  • Guaranteed analysis validation per batch
  • OMRI and CDFA organic input compliance for select SKUs
  • Labeling aligned with state fertilizer laws

Environmental Stewardship

Permitting, monitoring, and reporting ensure adherence to environmental regulations.

  • Stormwater Pollution Prevention Plans (SWPPP)
  • Odor and vector control SOPs with recordkeeping
  • Air quality and wastewater monitoring where required

Worker Safety & Biosecurity

Comprehensive safety programs protect staff and ensure biosecure operations.

  • OSHA-aligned training and lockout/tagout procedures
  • PPE protocols and hygiene zoning
  • Incident response plans and drills

4. Frass for Shrimp Pond Berms Opportunity

Overview: Salinity buildup (8-12 dS/m) stresses shrimp; frass on berms chelates Na, boosts bacteria, stabilizes pH (6.5-7.5). Texas A&M trial: +8% harvest, -10% salt.

Application: 1 ton/acre, 6" deep on pond edges. Not in water—soil buffer for quality.

Buyers: Danbury Farms (50 tons/yr, $200-300/ton); Bayou Blue (20 tons/yr).

Unit Econ: Input 1 ton post-waste → 400 lb frass → $100-160 revenue. Margin 35-60%.

Timeline

Milestones in Waste Valorization

2018

Laboratory Proof of Concept

Bench-scale trials confirm 70% reduction of pre-consumer food waste using BSFL with captured nutrient-rich frass.

2019

University Collaboration

Joint research with land-grant university quantifies larvae nutrient profiles and frass microbial communities.

2020

First Modular Pilot

Deployment at a regional grocery distribution center diverts 1,200 lbs/day with integrated QA monitoring.

2021

Regulatory Validation

Achieved feed ingredient registrations in initial states and delivered frass analytics to organic certification bodies.

2022

Frass Agronomy Network

Field trials with specialty crop growers demonstrate yield increases and soil carbon improvements.

2023

IoT & Data Integration

Launched remote monitoring stack that tracks climate, feed conversion, and system performance in real time.

2024

Commercial Partnerships

Signed multi-year agreements with beverage manufacturers and universities for on-site valorization units.

Research Backbone

Academic & Scientific Collaborations

Nutrient Cycling Studies

Partnership with land-grant universities to profile larvae amino acid composition and frass nutrient availability.

  • Peer-reviewed publications on BSFL feed efficiency
  • Isotope tracing demonstrates nutrient retention in soil
  • Data feeds into AAFCO and EU regulatory dossiers

Soil Health Research

Joint trials with regenerative ag institutes quantify frass impacts on microbial activity and mycorrhizal colonization.

  • Third-party analysis confirms improvements in aggregate stability
  • Comparative studies vs. compost and synthetic fertilizers
  • Data supports NRCS conservation practice standards

Animal Nutrition Trials

Feeds developed with aquaculture and poultry partners to validate performance and gut health benefits.

  • Higher survival rates in RAS tilapia trials
  • Improved feed conversion and egg quality in layer hens
  • Pathogen suppression linked to lauric acid-rich lipids

Lifecycle & Climate Analysis

Collaborations with climate scientists to measure emissions reductions and circularity metrics.

  • Lifecycle assessments benchmarking against landfilling and composting
  • Verified carbon diversion upwards of 3.2 tCO₂e per ton waste
  • Supports ESG reporting for partner facilities

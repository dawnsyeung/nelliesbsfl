Modular Technology & Infrastructure

Nellie’s modular units integrate climate control, automation, and data systems that keep larvae thriving year-round. We deliver turnkey hardware, software, and facility design tailored to your waste stream.

System Architecture

Inside Each Valorization Module

Climate-Controlled Rearing Racks

Multi-tier racks with automated airflow, humidity, and temperature regulation.

  • Variable speed fans and HEPA filtration
  • Humidification & dehumidification loops
  • UV-C sanitation between cohorts

Smart Feedstock Intake

Grinders, mixers, and conveyors designed for safe, efficient feedstock prep.

  • Load cells track mass balance and losses
  • Thermal kill step options for high-risk inputs
  • Automated dosing into larvae trays

Harvest & Finishing Suite

Separation, chilling, drying, and packaging integrated into a clean-zone workflow.

  • Fluidized bed or low-temp dryers
  • Frass screening & pelletizing equipment
  • Inline metal detection & packaging QA

Automation & Data

IoT Platform & Software Stack

Our supervisory control and data acquisition (SCADA) platform connects PLCs, sensors, and cameras to deliver actionable insights. Operators access dashboards on tablets for SOP guidance, alerts, and analytics.

Sensor suite: temperature, humidity, CO₂, NH₃, VOCs, vibration, power draw
AI anomaly detection for early warning on feed conversion and welfare
Batch traceability with QR-coded bins and lot-level reporting
API integrations with ERP, MES, and sustainability platforms

Data Services

  • Monthly performance optimization reviews
  • Custom dashboards for finance, ops, and ESG teams
  • Secure cloud storage with data ownership options

Infrastructure Requirements

Footprint, Utilities, & Facility Integration

Site Specifications

Modular units adapt to warehouses, loading docks, or outdoor enclosures.

  • Footprint per module: 1,200–2,500 sq ft
  • Clear height requirement: 16 ft minimum
  • Ambient temperature range: 50–95 °F (enclosed/insulated recommended)

Utility Profile

Utilities are engineered for efficiency and redundancy.

  • Electrical: 208/480V 3-phase, 75–150 amps per module
  • Water: 20–40 gpm supply, recirculated for sanitation
  • Wastewater: < 2 gpm average with solids separation
  • Optional heat reclaim integration

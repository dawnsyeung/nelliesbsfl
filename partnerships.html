Ideal Partners

Who Benefits From On-Site Valorization

Food Manufacturers

Bakeries, produce processors, and ready-meal producers divert high-volume organic by-products.

  • Eliminates landfill tipping fees
  • Turns QC rejects into animal feed inputs
  • Supports zero-waste brand commitments

Grocery & Distribution Centers

Regional distribution hubs manage expired or damaged goods with minimal hauling.

  • Reduces transport miles and associated emissions
  • Frass returns to supplier farms to close nutrient loops
  • Compliance with state organics diversion mandates

Universities & Campuses

Dining hall waste and research programs integrate into living labs.

  • Engages students in circular economy projects
  • Offsets composting costs with frass for campus landscaping
  • Aligns with climate action and zero waste plans

Breweries & Beverage Producers

Spent grain, yeast, and fruit pulp convert to larvae feed without spoilage concerns.

  • Consumes wet residues before they sour
  • Larvae meal sold back to partner farms or feed mills
  • Heat capture integration options

Municipal Transfer Stations

Cities meet organics diversion laws while creating local employment.

  • Modular units fit existing waste management footprints
  • Frass supports urban agriculture and parks
  • Eligible for environmental grant funding

Livestock & Aquaculture Farms

On-farm installations recycle manure-adjacent feedstocks into feed and soil amendments.

  • Reduces feed import reliance
  • Improves manure management and odor control
  • Frass reinvested into pasture and crop fertility

Modular Units

How Our On-Site Systems Work

Each deployment uses climate-controlled modules sized to your waste stream. Units include feedstock intake, larvae rearing racks, harvest stations, and frass finishing equipment. Remote monitoring dashboards give stakeholders real-time visibility into throughput, quality, and savings.

Daily processing capacity from 0.5 to 10 tons per module cluster
Automated climate management for larvae welfare
Built-in QA lab for nutrient, moisture, and pathogen checks
Predictive maintenance and uptime tracking

Value Snapshot

  • Waste hauling reduction: 35–80%
  • New revenue from larvae & frass sales
  • Scope 3 emissions reductions for ESG goals
  • Co-branded storytelling & visitor engagement

Environmental & Waste Regulations

We navigate state and municipal organics policies, permitting, and biosolids guidelines.

Feed & Fertilizer Compliance

Larvae ingredients and frass products carry the required registrations and QA protocols.

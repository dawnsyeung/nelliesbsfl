Closed-Loop Operations

Valorization Process

Every kilogram of organic residuals is mapped, conditioned, and transformed through a 14-day program that yields nutrient-dense larvae and biologically active frass. Our modular units pair precise bioconversion with real-time analytics to guarantee repeatable outcomes.

Traceable • Biosecure • Data-Driven

Stage by Stage

From Residual to Revenue

The valorization line combines intuitive operator tasks with automated controls. Each stage is tracked through our production console, ensuring HACCP, biosafety, and performance metrics are captured.

1. Intake & Profiling

Incoming organics are scanned, weighed, and assigned a nutrient profile before entering the clean zone.

  • Digital manifests & generator analytics
  • Rapid moisture + pH testing
  • Foreign material removal and documentation

2. Pre-Processing

Feedstocks are milled, blended, or pasteurized to align with larvae dietary targets.

  • Particle size reduction & homogenization
  • Thermal kill steps for risk materials
  • Batching for optimal C:N ratios

3. Inoculation & Ramp-Up

Starter larvae populations are paired with conditioned feed in sealed rearing trays.

  • Automated larvae dosing per tray
  • Lot tracking with QR-coded inventory
  • Early-life density monitoring

4. Growth & Stewardship

Climate-controlled racks keep larvae within optimal humidity, airflow, and thermal bands.

  • IoT sensors for temperature, CO₂, NH₃ and VOCs
  • Automated misting & aeration schedules
  • Benchmarks for feed conversion and weight gain

5. Harvest & Separation

Larvae are separated from frass using vibration screens and density sorting.

  • Humane cold-stunning before processing
  • Fractionating juvenile larvae for seed stock
  • Inline moisture adjustment for both outputs

6. Finishing & Packaging

Outputs are stabilized to meet product specifications and packaged with full traceability.

  • Drying, pressing, or pelletizing modules
  • Lot-coded labelling and palletization
  • QA release tied to retain samples

Process Governance

Controls That Protect Every Batch

Our operating system integrates with on-unit PLCs and handheld devices to surface real-time alerts. Production Rhythm

Fourteen-Day Valorization Timeline

Day 0

Feedstock Certification

Approved organics dispatched from partner sites and staged inside the conditioned inbound bay.

Days 1-3

Conditioning + Inoculation

Blended diets paired with neonate larvae; densitometry and moisture calibrations logged.

Days 4-9

Accelerated Growth

Larvae reach peak feed conversion; climate data automatically recorded every 15 minutes.

Days 10-11

Harvest Preparation

Bins rotated for final purging; feed withheld for 18 hours to enhance gut clearance.

Days 12-13

Separation & Finishing

Larvae separated, chilled, and directed to oil pressing or meal drying; frass is screened and equilibrated.

Day 14

QA Release & Dispatch

Lot certificates issued, inventory synced with ERP, and logistics bookings triggered.

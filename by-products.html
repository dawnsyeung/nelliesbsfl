Product Library

Outputs Optimized for Multiple Markets

Defatted Protein Meal

High-protein, low-fat ingredient with a balanced amino acid profile and excellent digestibility.

  • Crude protein 58-62% with <5% ash
  • Ideal for poultry, aquaculture, and swine formulations
  • Processed in SQF-aligned facility with pathogen testing

Whole Dried Larvae

Slow-dried to preserve lipids and lauric acid for backyard poultry, reptiles, and specialty feed.

  • Natural calcium-to-phosphorus ratio of 2.7:1
  • Crunch-stable packaging for direct-to-consumer markets
  • Optional probiotic coatings for premium SKUs

Larval Lipid Concentrate

Clarified insect oil rich in lauric (C12) and myristic (C14) acids with antimicrobial properties.

  • Cold-pressed and triple-filtered
  • Applications in feed, cosmetics, and industrial biolubes
  • Oxidation index < 5 with natural antioxidant stabilization

Frass Soil Amendments

Microbial-rich fertilizer containing chitin, beneficial microbes, and plant-available nutrients.

  • Guaranteed NPK 4-2-3 with high micronutrient bioavailability
  • Carrier options: crumbles, pellets, or biochar blends
  • Validated for organic production inputs

Chitin & Frass Extracts

Fractionated to deliver biostimulant compounds for foliar sprays and seed treatments.

  • Water-extracted polysaccharides with consistent molecular weight
  • Supports induced systemic resistance in row crops
  • Compatible with fertigation and drip systems

Digestate Tea Concentrate

Liquid concentrate derived from frass steeping for greenhouse and hydroponic operations.

  • High microbial counts with shelf-stable formulations
  • Carbohydrate-microbe pairing boosts rhizosphere health
  • Available with mycorrhizal consortia boosters
